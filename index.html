ABOUT ME

Hello! I am a general dentist and software developer with a passion for healthcare technologies!
I was born and raised in the Silicon Valley and decided to pursue college abroad in India. I graduated with a degree in dentistry from Kamineni Institue of Dental Sciences, Dr.NTR University of Health Sciences in 2018.
I worked as a dental assistant in California as I gained my credentials here to pursue a specialization in Pediatric dentistry.
Throughout my career in dentistry, I have been curious about the dental technology and software I worked with that enabled me to provide quality and efficient patient care. During the pandemic, I started exploring and developing a passion for software development and its application in healthcare. My future goal is to work on dental/medical software development, and I am excited to optimize these tools to my fellow clinicians' needs.
I have graduated from a Full-Stack Software Engineering Bootcamp at App Academy, known for its 5% acceptance rate and rigorous curriculum with over 1000+ of instructional training. I have built several full-stack applications using React, Redux, Python, Flask, Javascript, Express, SQL, PostgresSQL, Sequelize, SQLAlchemy, HTML, CSS, and AWS. I have also worked with Mocha and Chai testing frameworks as well. We focussed on SCRUM methodologies, tech etiquette, pair programming, and Agile principles.
I am passionate about classical arts and I pursue dance professionally. When I am not coding, I enjoy cooking and exploring the natural beauty that California has to offer. I promote living organ donation awareness and participate in fundraising events whenever possible.
